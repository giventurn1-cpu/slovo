15–30 seconds around the strongest part of the track is what performs on Reels and TikTok. Trim to the hook, add the drop moment, export — your teaser is ready before the coffee is.

How to promote a song on TikTok and Reels

Post a native vertical clip, not a screenshot of your cover. A moving scene with lyrics holds attention longer, and longer holds mean more reach. Make three different teasers from the same track — different hooks, different visuals — and let the platform tell you which one works.
