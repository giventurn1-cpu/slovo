What is a Spotify Canvas?

A Canvas is the short looping video that plays behind your song in the Spotify app instead of the static cover. Artists use it because it makes a track feel alive — and Spotify has said Canvas can lift streams and saves. To upload one in Spotify for Artists it has to match a strict spec:

Aspect ratio9:16 (vertical)
Length3–8 seconds, loops seamlessly
Resolution720×1280 or higher
FileMP4, no sound

SLOVO's Canvas mode exports exactly this — you don't have to worry about the numbers.

How to make a Spotify Canvas for free

  1. Open slovo.video in Chrome or Edge on your computer.
  2. Load your track — drop an MP3 or WAV. It stays on your device; nothing is uploaded.
  3. Pick a look — choose a beat-reactive visual, or use your own photo or video loop as the background. Add subtitles if you want lyrics on screen.
  4. Export → Canvas — save a silent 3–8s, 9:16 MP4, then upload it in Spotify for Artists.
